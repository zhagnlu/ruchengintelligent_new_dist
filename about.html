About Rucheng Technology

Your Trusted Partner in Sheet Metal Fabrication

Company Overview

Nanjing Rucheng Technology Co., Ltd. is a modern management enterprise that produces all kinds of ordinary and CNC bending machines, plate shears, laser cutting machines and supporting knives and molds. We have a number of knowledgeable and young teams dedicated to the development of sheet metal processing machine tools.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the sheet metal fabrication industry, Rucheng Technology has grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer of precision machinery. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for businesses worldwide.

Our Mission
