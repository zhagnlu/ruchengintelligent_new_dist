Key Components

Understanding the core components that deliver precision laser cutting performance and reliability

Fiber Laser Source

High-power fiber laser generator (multiple power options) from leading manufacturers like Raycus or IPG. Offers exceptional beam quality, high electro-optical efficiency up to 30%, and maintenance-free operation with 100,000+ hour lifespan. Stable output ensures consistent cutting quality.

CNC Control System

Advanced CNC system (CypCut or Raytools) with intuitive interface for easy programming and operation. Supports complex path planning, automatic nesting, and real-time monitoring. Compatible with standard file formats (DXF, DWG) for seamless CAD/CAM integration.

Auto-Focus Cutting Head

Professional auto-focus cutting head with capacitive height sensor for automatic focal length adjustment. Ensures optimal focus position across different material thicknesses. Built-in collision protection and quick lens replacement design minimize downtime.

Linear Motor Drive System

High-precision linear motor or servo motor drive system with precision ball screws and linear guides. Provides fast acceleration, high positioning accuracy (High precision), and smooth motion control. Dual-drive design ensures synchronized movement and long-term stability.

Double Exchange Table System

Automatic shuttle table exchange system with two independent Customizable working tables. Rapid exchange time powered by precision servo motors. Pneumatic clamping system secures materials during cutting. One table processes parts while the other loads/unloads, maximizing productivity and eliminating idle time for continuous 24/7 operation capability.

Assist Gas & Cooling System

Precision gas delivery system with automatic pressure control for oxygen, nitrogen, or air assist. Industrial water chiller maintains optimal laser source temperature. Dust extraction system ensures clean cutting environment and protects optical components from contamination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our fiber laser cutting machines

Q:How much faster is a double table machine compared to single table?

Double table machines typically achieve 40-50% higher productivity than single table machines. The automatic table exchange takes only rapid, and while one table is cutting, the operator can load/unload the other table simultaneously. This eliminates material loading downtime and enables continuous 24/7 operation, making it ideal for high-volume production environments.

Q:What materials and thicknesses can be cut?

Our fiber laser cutting machines can cut various thicknesses of carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and 12mm, and copper/brass up to 8mm (thicknesses vary based on laser power). Higher wattage lasers can cut thicker materials. The machines handle various metals including mild steel, stainless steel, galvanized steel, aluminum alloys, copper, brass, and titanium.

Q:What maintenance does a fiber laser require?

Fiber lasers are nearly maintenance-free compared to CO2 lasers. Regular maintenance includes: cleaning protective lenses weekly, checking water chiller levels, inspecting gas pressure, and cleaning dust extraction filters. The fiber laser source itself requires no maintenance and has a lifespan of 100,000+ hours. No laser gas refills or mirror alignments are needed.

Q:Is training and installation support provided?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support including on-site installation, machine commissioning, and operator training (typically 3-5 days). Training covers machine operation, CNC programming, parameter optimization, basic maintenance, and troubleshooting. Complete operation manuals and video tutorials are also provided.

Q:What are the operating costs?

Operating costs include electricity (fiber lasers are highly efficient with ~30% electro-optical conversion), assist gas (nitrogen, oxygen, or compressed air), water chiller electricity, and occasional consumables (protective lenses, nozzles). Fiber lasers are more cost-effective than CO2 lasers due to lower power consumption and minimal maintenance requirements.

Q:What is the expected delivery time?

Standard configuration machines typically ship within 30-45 days after order confirmation. Custom specifications or higher power configurations may require 45-60 days. We provide regular production updates and coordinate logistics to ensure smooth delivery and installation.
