Key Components

Understanding the core components that deliver precision tube cutting performance and reliability

Fiber Laser Source

High-power fiber laser generator (multiple power options) from leading manufacturers like Raycus or IPG. Offers exceptional beam quality, high electro-optical efficiency up to 30%, and maintenance-free operation with 100,000+ hour lifespan. Stable output ensures consistent cutting quality across all tube materials.

Four-Chuck Clamping System

Advanced four-chuck pneumatic clamping system provides stable tube positioning during cutting. Minimizes vibration and prevents tube displacement, ensuring precise cuts and tight tolerances. Quick-release design allows rapid tube changes for efficient production. Compatible with various tube shapes and sizes.

Automatic Tube Feeding System

Bundle feeder system holds up to 1800kg of raw tube stock for continuous operation. Automatically feeds tubes one by one into the cutting area, eliminating manual loading and maximizing productivity. Programmable for different tube sizes and materials. Reduces operator intervention and increases throughput.

SmartCut AI Control System

Intelligent CNC control system with real-time parameter optimization. Automatically adjusts laser power, cutting speed, and focus position based on material characteristics and tube geometry. Reduces scrap by up to 18% through intelligent analysis. User-friendly interface supports complex pattern programming and 3D visualization.

Rotary Chuck & Drive System

High-precision rotary drive system enables 360¬∞ tube rotation for complete circumferential cutting. Servo motor control provides smooth acceleration and accurate positioning. Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tube laser cutting machines

Q:What tube shapes and sizes can the machine cut?

Our tube laser cutting machines can process round, square, rectangular, and oval tubes. Standard capacity ranges from customizable diameter, with optional upgrades available for tubes larger diameter options. The machine can handle tube lengths up to Customizable length (standard) with extended options up to 12000mm. Wall thicknesses depend on laser power and material type.

Q:How does the automatic tube feeding system work?

The bundle feeder system can hold up to 1800kg of raw tube stock arranged in bundles. Tubes are automatically separated and fed one by one into the four-chuck clamping system. The system is programmable for different tube sizes and can operate continuously without manual intervention. This dramatically increases productivity by eliminating manual loading time and allowing operators to focus on other tasks.

Q:What is the advantage of the four-chuck clamping system?

The four-chuck clamping system provides superior stability compared to three-chuck systems. It minimizes vibration during cutting, prevents tube displacement, and ensures consistent positioning accuracy of High precision. This is especially important for thin-walled tubes and complex cutting patterns. The pneumatic system allows for quick tube changes while maintaining precise clamping force across different tube sizes.

Q:Can the machine cut bevels for pipe welding?

Yes, with the optional bevel cutting head upgrade, our tube laser can cut various bevel angles for pipe welding preparation. The system can create V-grooves, K-grooves, and other weld joint configurations at precise angles. This eliminates the need for secondary beveling operations and ensures perfect fit-up for welding. The SmartCut AI system automatically calculates optimal cutting paths for complex bevel geometries.

Q:What materials can be cut with the tube laser?

The fiber laser tube cutter can process carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, and titanium tubes. Maximum cutting thickness varies by material: carbon steel up to 8mm, stainless steel up to 6mm, aluminum up to 5mm (thicknesses depend on laser power). The machine handles both coated and uncoated tubes, including galvanized and powder-coated materials.

Q:What is SmartCut AI and how does it improve cutting?

SmartCut AI is our intelligent control system that automatically optimizes cutting parameters in real-time. It analyzes tube geometry, material characteristics, and wall thickness to adjust laser power, cutting speed, and focal position. This reduces material waste by up to 18%, improves edge quality, and minimizes operator skill requirements. The system also includes automatic nesting software to maximize material utilization.
