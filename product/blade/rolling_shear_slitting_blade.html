Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our rolling shear slitting blades

Q:How long do rolling shear blades typically last?

Blade life depends on material being cut, cutting speed, and maintenance practices. Our HSS blades typically last 5-10 times longer than standard blades. In metal slitting applications, expect hundreds of thousands of linear meters before re-sharpening is needed. Proper lubrication and regular inspection extend blade life significantly.

Q:Can these blades be re-sharpened?

Yes, our rolling shear blades are designed for multiple re-sharpening cycles. Professional re-grinding can restore cutting performance to near-original condition. Most blades can be re-sharpened 3-5 times or more, depending on wear patterns and application. We offer re-sharpening services with quick turnaround times.

Q:What materials can be cut with these blades?

Our rolling shear blades handle a wide range of materials including steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, brass, plastic films, paper, cardboard, rubber, foam, textiles, and composite materials. Material thickness and hardness determine optimal blade specification. Contact us for application-specific recommendations.

Q:How do I select the correct blade size?

Blade selection depends on your machine specifications, material type, and cutting requirements. Key factors include outer diameter (matching machine arbor spacing), bore size (matching arbor diameter), and blade thickness (based on slitting precision needs). Our technical team can help specify the optimal configuration for your application.

Q:What is the typical lead time for custom blades?

Standard size blades typically ship within 7-10 business days. Custom configurations require 2-4 weeks depending on specifications and quantity. Large volume orders may require additional production time. We maintain inventory of popular sizes for immediate shipment. Rush orders can be accommodated in many cases.

Q:Do you offer technical support and installation guidance?

Yes, comprehensive technical support is included with all blade orders. We provide installation guidelines, setup recommendations, troubleshooting assistance, and optimization advice. Our technical team can help with blade selection, machine setup, and performance optimization. Training materials and best practices documentation are available.
