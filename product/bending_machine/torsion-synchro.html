Components

Understanding the key components that make our torsion-synchro press brake reliable and efficient

Mechanical Structure

The main frame is constructed from high-quality welded steel, providing excellent rigidity and stability. The torsion bar synchronization system ensures parallel movement of the ram, delivering consistent bending results across the entire working length.

Torsion Bar Synchronization

The torsion bar synchronization system is a proven mechanical solution that ensures the left and right sides of the ram move in perfect synchronization. This simple yet effective system requires minimal maintenance and provides reliable long-term performance.

NC Control System

User-friendly NC controller with digital display for easy operation. Features include programmable back gauge positioning, stroke adjustment, and bending counter. The intuitive interface allows operators to quickly set up bending parameters.

NC Back Gauge

2-axis NC back gauge system provides accurate positioning for consistent bending results. The motorized back gauge eliminates manual adjustment, improving productivity and reducing setup time. Ball screw drive ensures smooth and precise movement.

Hydraulic System

Reliable hydraulic system with quality components ensures consistent bending force. The system includes a hydraulic pump, control valves, and cylinders designed for efficient operation and long service life. Oil cooling system prevents overheating during continuous operation.

Safety System

Essential safety features including emergency stop buttons, safety guards, and two-hand control operation. The machine meets CE safety standards to protect operators during all phases of operation while maintaining productivity and ease of use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our torsion-synchro NC press brakes

Q:What is the difference between torsion-synchro and hydraulic synchronization?

Torsion-synchro uses a mechanical torsion bar to synchronize the ram movement, making it simpler and more economical than hydraulic or electro-hydraulic systems. While it offers slightly less precision than electro-hydraulic systems, it's more reliable and requires less maintenance.

Q:What materials can be bent with this machine?

The torsion-synchro press brake can process mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and other common metal alloys. The maximum thickness depends on the material type, tonnage, and bending length.

Q:How much maintenance does it require?

Very minimal maintenance is required. Regular hydraulic oil changes, lubrication of moving parts, and occasional inspection of the torsion bar are typically all that's needed. The mechanical design is inherently reliable.

Q:Is it suitable for beginners?

Yes, the torsion-synchro press brake is ideal for operators with basic training. The simple NC controller and straightforward operation make it easy to learn compared to more complex CNC systems.

Q:What is the typical lead time?

Standard models typically ship within 20-30 days. Custom configurations may require 45-60 days. Contact us for specific delivery timelines.

Q:What warranty is included?

We provide a standard 1-year warranty covering all major components. Extended warranty options are available. Technical support is provided for the life of the machine.
