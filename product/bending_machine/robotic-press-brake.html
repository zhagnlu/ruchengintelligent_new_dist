Components

Understand the advanced components that make our robotic press brake the most sophisticated automated bending solution

6-Axis Industrial Robot

High-precision 6-axis industrial robot with advanced servo drives and absolute encoders. Features adaptive gripping technology, vision-guided positioning, and intelligent path planning for optimal material handling. The robot's high payload capacity and extended reach enable handling of large sheets and complex part geometries.

AI-Powered Control System

Advanced artificial intelligence control system with adaptive bending algorithms, real-time angle measurement, and automatic springback compensation. The system continuously learns and optimizes bending parameters based on material characteristics and historical data, ensuring perfect bends every time.

3D Vision & Positioning System

High-resolution 3D vision system with advanced image processing for precise part recognition, orientation detection, and quality inspection. The system guides the robot for accurate part pickup and positioning, while simultaneously performing in-process quality checks for bend angles and dimensions.

Automatic Tool Change System

Fully automated tool changing system with intelligent tool magazine that stores multiple punch and die sets. The system automatically selects and changes tools based on programmed bending sequences, eliminating manual setup time and enabling rapid transition between different part programs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our robotic press brake systems

Q:What are the main advantages of a robotic press brake?

Robotic press brakes offer significant advantages including 24/7 unmanned operation, consistent quality, significantly increased productivity, reduced labor costs, enhanced safety by removing operators from hazardous zones, and the ability to automate complex multi-bend sequences.

Q:How long does it take to program new parts?

With our advanced offline programming software and 3D simulation, new parts can be programmed quickly without interrupting production. The AI system also learns from each bending operation to optimize future programs automatically.

Q:What is the ROI for a robotic press brake system?

Most customers see ROI quickly through labor savings, increased productivity, reduced scrap rates, and the ability to run unmanned shifts. The exact ROI depends on your production volume, part complexity, and current labor costs.

Q:Do I need special skills to operate a robotic system?

Our system features an intuitive interface that requires minimal robotics knowledge. We provide comprehensive training for operators and programmers. Most operators become proficient with basic operations within a short training period.

Q:Can the system handle different sheet sizes and materials?

Yes, our robotic system is highly flexible and can handle a wide range of sheet sizes and materials. The adaptive gripper system automatically adjusts for different part geometries, and the AI control adapts bending parameters for different materials including steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.

Q:What maintenance is required?

The system includes predictive maintenance capabilities that alert you before issues occur. Routine maintenance is minimal - primarily gripper inspection, robot lubrication (automated), and standard press brake maintenance. We provide remote diagnostics and support.
